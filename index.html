Our Story

Meeting in grace, growing in faith

We met in November 2025, on an evening made for lingering conversations. What began as coffee and a shared curiosity became a rhythm of evenings at the opera and quiet awe at the ballet — two hearts learning the same language of beauty.

Juliette's faith has been a gentle, steady light. In her witness — in the way she prays, hopes, and loves — Jimmy found a home he hadn't known he was searching for. Inspired by her, Jimmy has begun his own spiritual journey.

It is the greatest joy of our engagement, and the foundation of our marriage. We now come to the altar not only to pledge ourselves to one another, but to place our love within the heart of the Church, beneath the soaring vaults where heaven seems close enough to touch.
